# Welcome to RamBros Docs

RamBros 3D is an open-source project focused on creating highly detailed and accurate **3D printable scale models**. Since 2021, we have been developing models ranging from straightforward builds to complex, mechanically advanced models that test the practical limits of 3D printing technology.

---

## Our Projects

### Dragon Railway

An open-source **3D printable model railway ecosystem** in S-scale (1/64), featuring locomotives, wagons, tracks, and control systems.

- Fully 3D printed components
- Parametric track design
- Auto couplers and compact bogies
- Multiple scales: S-scale (1/64) and G-scale (1/32)

[Explore Dragon Railway →](/railway/)

---

### Dragon Trucks

An open-source **3D printable model automobile ecosystem** in S-scale (1/64), compatible with the Dragon Railway ecosystem.

- DAF CF85 truck variants
- Flatbed, box, and container trailers
- Compatible with Dragon Railway tracks

[Explore Dragon Trucks →](/trucks/)

---

## Getting Started

1. **Choose a project** — Decide between Dragon Railway or Dragon Trucks
2. **Download files** — Get the 3D models from [MakerWorld](https://makerworld.com) or [Printables](https://www.printables.com/@rambros/collections)
3. **Print & build** — Follow the assembly manuals
4. **Add electronics** — Use our control boards or DIY wiring

---

## Open Source

Most of our designs are **open source or free**. We choose to make our models open source because we like others to benefit from our work, just as we have from the works of other people.
