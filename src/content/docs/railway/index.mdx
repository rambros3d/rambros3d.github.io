The Dragon Railway is an open-source **3D printable model railway ecosystem** in S-scale (1/64). This project is focused on creating a highly **detailed and accurate scale model** of a railway system, complete with locomotives, wagons/rail cars, tracks, control systems and other accessories.

## Fully 3D Printed

As we say **"If it can be 3D printed, it will be 3D printed";** From the body and wheels to the couplers, **every major component is 3D printed**. The only additional parts you'll need to purchase are a few screws, bearings, O-rings, and the necessary electronics.

## Inception 2022

**Dragon Railway first started as a HO scale project.**

The idea of a fully 3D printed ecosystem first had its inception in **early 2022**. We wanted to make a HO scale railway system, but after a lot of testing we came to the conclusion that it was **too unreliable** to print with FDM printers. So we then changed the scale to **1/64, everything was much better.** It was fine in its early days. We experimented with various motors, even tried some BLDC motors. By V1 it was running very smooth and reliable. Then we designed the other parts such as autocoupler, wagons, turnouts and everything that has made Dragon Railway today.

**Each of these components have a story of its own, but this is the super simplified history of this project.**

The designs for the HO scale is available here for your reference: https://github.com/DragonRailway/ho-scale-v0
